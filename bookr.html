Book Review: Psychology of Money.

Author: Morgan Housel, Pradnes Gujrathi.
Genre: Personal Finance / Psychology.
Published: 2020.
Pages: 252
⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)


The Psychology of Money
explores how emotions, biases, and behavior shape our financial decisions—often more than pure logic and technical knowledge. Morgan Housel shares timeless lessons through compelling storytelling, drawing on history, psychology, and his own experiences.

One of its key insights is that financial success doesn't depend on knowing complex formulas, but on how you behave—how patient, frugal, and consistent you are. The book emphasizes the power of long-term thinking and the unpredictability of markets, making it a must-read not just for investors but anyone who interacts with money.

This book is a refreshing take on wealth-building that celebrates humility, self-awareness, and the emotional intelligence behind financial decisions. Highly recommended for beginners and experts alike. Read It If You Are A Barca Fan.
